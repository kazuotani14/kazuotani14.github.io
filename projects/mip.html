Mobile Inverted Pendulum Robot

This was a project for MAE 143C (Digital Controls) at UCSD. I designed lead-lag controllers with successive loop closure, based on a simplified dynamical model. Tuned controllers and IMU filters on MATLAB, converted to difference equations and implemented in C on Debian. Added support for Wifi dongle for remote control, 3D printed an attachment for a webcam to stream video over Wifi. Video of simple balancing available here.
